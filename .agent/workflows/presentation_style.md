description: How to maintain the presentation style
---

## 1. Visual Consistency
- **Colors**: Use the official CineFlix Red (`#E50914`) for primary emphasis (titles, key metrics) to mimic the Netflix brand. Use Dark Blue/Black (`#0A0A1F`) for backgrounds or heavy text to maintain the app's theme.
- **Imagery**: Always mix "Streaming" visuals (movie posters, UI mockups) with "Data" visuals (server icons, database cylinders).

## 2. Slide Structure (Academic/Technical)
- **Left/Right Split**: Place text on the Left, Visuals on the Right (mirroring the friend's successful template).
- **Hierarchy**:
  1.  **Title**: Bold, Sans-serif (e.g., Roboto or Inter).
  2.  **Subtitle**: Descriptive/Technical.
  3.  **Body**: Bullet points (Max 4-5 per slide).

## 3. Recurring Elements
- **Logos**: Keep University logos at the top (smaller, aligned).
- **Footer**: "CINEFLIX - [Your Name]" at the bottom right in small grey text.
